Tobo is the beloved mascot of Force of Gravy, our senior year animation team.

FIRST Robotics Safety Animation

I, along with my trusted companions Addison Eyster, Ayo Akinbo, and Josh Mullen created this as a submission for the annual FIRST Robotics Safety animation competition.  We put a lot of blood, sweat, and tears into this (mostly tears because the computers crashed every few minutes).  After a lot of technical headaches, we finally managed to get something decent-looking out.

We use raytracing and physically accurate models and materials to create photorealistic results.  You'll be able to see this below in how some of the higher-quality renderings look just like photographs.
